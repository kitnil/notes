K-Pop, also known as Korean pop music, is a popular music genre that originated in South Korea. Here are some of the most popular female K-Pop groups:

BLACKPINK: BLACKPINK is a four-member girl group that debuted in 2016. They are known for their energetic performances, stylish fashion, and catchy pop songs.

Twice: Twice is a nine-member girl group that debuted in 2015. They are known for their cheerful and bright pop music, catchy choruses, and synchronized dance performances.

Red Velvet: Red Velvet is a five-member girl group that debuted in 2014. They are known for their unique sound that combines elements of pop, hip-hop, and R&B and their visually stunning music videos.

Girls' Generation (SNSD): Girls' Generation, also known as SNSD, is a nine-member girl group that debuted in 2007. They are considered one of the leading girl groups in K-Pop and are known for their strong vocals and visually stunning music videos.

Wonder Girls: Wonder Girls is a five-member girl group that debuted in 2007. They are considered one of the pioneers of the K-Pop industry and are known for their strong vocals and catchy pop music.

Mamamoo: Mamamoo is a four-member girl group that debuted in 2014. They are known for their powerful vocals, retro-inspired sound, and charismatic performances.

These are just a few of the most popular female K-Pop groups, and the list is constantly evolving as new groups debut and existing groups gain popularity. K-Pop has a global following and has been increasingly gaining popularity in recent years, especially among female fans.
